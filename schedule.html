☆ TRAIN SCHEDULE! ☆

The MRT train schedule remains fairly consistent, except when there are technical issues or unexpected delays. Please be patient and stay informed through official announcements.


PASSENGER REMINDERS

  • Arrive at the station 15 minutes before your scheduled departure.
  • Ensure you have your ticket or contactless card ready for scanning.
  • Observe all safety protocols, including wearing masks and social distancing where required.
  • Report any suspicious activities to the nearest station officer immediately.
  • Keep your personal belongings with you at all times. Do not leave items unattended.
  • For any delays or cancellations, check the official announcements on the station's digital boards or website.
